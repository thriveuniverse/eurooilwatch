Methodology

Score = scenario-adjusted probability × impact, normalised 0–10. Adjusted probability = your base estimate (read as the “fragile / current” state) scaled by the master-variable multiplier for that node’s war-sensitivity, capped at 95%. Bars and the rail colour shade by severity. The stress index is the mean of the top three scores — a deliberately blunt heuristic, since the nodes are correlated and a true joint model would need a dependency structure.
